About Me

First and foremost, I love writing code. Ever since writing my first "Hello World" in C, I have been passionated about building software that improves the lives of people around the world.

This passion carried me through my education at Dublin City University where I studied different technologies, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics.

The passion remains with me in industry, I've worked as a backend developer on messaging systems, commercial APIs, event based systems, web services for the past 8 years. Currently, I work for Zalando as a scala developer based in Dublin, soaking up as much as knowledge and practice as I can in an attempt to perfect my craft.
